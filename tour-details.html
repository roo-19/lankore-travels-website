Useful Information

Key Notes

  • Guide: A Friendly and professional tour leader tasked with ensuring the smooth operations of the tour, as well as providing practical support to passengers throughout the entire trip.
  • Transport: Private transportation in an fully air-conditioned modern vehicle.
  • Flight: International flights are excluded. You can organize your flights separately. We’ll arrange free airport pick-up and drop-off.

Important Notes

  • Most entrance and activity fees are paid in local currency. We suggest you keep some Sri Lankan rupees.
  • Religious places have a strict protocol when it comes to clothes and people should cover themselves up.
  • Sun protection, mosquito repellent, sunglasses and a hat can be very useful during your tour.
  • The official check-in time at all hotels will be at 02:00 pm. The official check-out time is at 11:00 am.

Climate And Average Temperatures

Sri Lanka is a year-around holiday destination with two monsoon seasons. The temperature is constantly around 27°C on the coast, while on the hill side it always stays below 20°C.

Terms And Conditions

  • Children must be accompanied by a responsible adult 18 years old or over to travel. No unaccompanied minors allowed.
  • No refund for unused services.
