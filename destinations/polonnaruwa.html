About Polonnaruwa

Following the decline of Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa became the second royal capital of Sri Lanka. Today, it remains one of the best-planned archeological relic sites in the country, testifying to the discipline and greatness of the Kingdom's first rulers. Explore the monumental Parakrama Samudra reservoir, the stunning Gal Vihara statues carved from solid granite, and the extensive palace ruins.

Visiting Polonnaruwa offers a unique blend of heritage, natural beauty, and unforgettable experiences that perfectly embody the spirit of Sri Lanka.

Top Places to Visit

  • Parakrama Samudra
  • Royal Palace Ruins
  • Vatadage
  • Rankoth Vehera
