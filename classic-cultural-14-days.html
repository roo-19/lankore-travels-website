Overview

Two Weeks of Grand Journey in Sri Lanka

This incredible 14-day Sri Lanka itinerary takes in the very best that the island has to offer. You will enjoy an epic journey from the bustling city of Negombo to the ancient ruins and temples that make up the Cultural Triangle. Travel through the eastern coast of the island and enjoy the calmness of blissful hidden beaches. Enjoy wildlife safari where you could spot sloth bears or leopards and watch hundreds of elephants feeding and bathing in a lake.

In addition, visit the mystical Hill Country, full of tea plantations and colonial hill stations. Learn how tea is made and explore gorgeous villages in the mountains.

Then, head down south to the coast, take part in whale-watching tours, try your skills at surfing board and eat some of the best seafood on the entire island. Lastly, cap off a wonderful holiday in the UNESCO World Heritage site of Galle Fort, browsing through galleries and handcraft shops, sipping on a chill beer and watching the sunset from the fort ramparts.

Itinerary

We have carefully planned out each day to give you the best possible experience. From exploring historic landmarks to tasting delicious local cuisine, each day is packed with adventure and excitement. Join us as we take you on a journey through some of the most fascinating destinations in the world.

DAY 1 - AIRPORT PICK-UP / NEGOMBO

Welcome to Sri Lanka, a land like no other, where this nine days tour of discovery will expose you to the country’s rich history and culture, as well as its wildlife, soft adventures, and secluded beaches with stunning golden sand.

You will be met by your tour guide when you arrive at Colombo CMB Airport, and you will then be taken to your accommodation. The first stop will be the seaside town of Negombo, which has long been regarded as Sri Lanka’s most well-preserved beach and is well-known for its cuisine, entertainment, and traditional fishery village. We haven’t scheduled any activities for day one, so you can unwind at Negombo Beach or by the pool of your cozy hotel. You can walk along the beach in the evening while enjoying the beautiful sunsetting and the relaxing atmosphere of Negombo beach.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Arrival to Sri Lanka, Colombo (Katunayaka) International Airport
  • Greetings by Lankore Travels representative
  • Proceed to Negombo
  • Check into Hotel in Negombo
  • Walk around the Negombo city
  • Relax at the beach and pool of your hotel
DAY 2 - KINGDOM OF ANURADHAPURA / WILPATTU SAFARI

Anuradhapura, city, north-central Sri Lanka. It is situated along the Aruvi Aru River. The old section of Anuradhapura, now preserved as an archaeological park and designated a UNESCO World Heritage site in 1982, is the best known of Sri Lanka’s ancient ruined cities.

In the immediate vicinity are huge bell-shaped dagobas (Buddhist commemorative shrines, or stupas) built of small sun-dried bricks as well as temples, sculptures, palaces, and ancient drinking-water reservoirs. The city also contains an ancient pipal tree that is believed to have originally been a branch of the Bo tree at Bodh Gaya (Bihar, India), under which Gautama Buddha attained Enlightenment.

Wilpttu National park is among the oldest and most important protected areas in Sri Lanka. It also contains a number of important cultural sites. The sanctuary lies inland from the coast and is entirely within Northern Province.

ACTIVITIES:
  • After breakfast proceed towards Anuradhapura
  • Visit Sri Maha Bodhi and Ruwanwali seya
  • A tour around the ancient Anuradhapura city
  • Evening join with the Wilpattu National Park safari
DAY 3 & 4 - TRINCOMALEE / EASTERN COAST

Day 3: After breakfast, you’ll travel towards the eastern coast of Sri Lanka and you will visit the city of Trincomalee. It is an amazing multicultural town with lovely places such as Nilaveli Beach, Pigeon Island and Koneswaram Kovil. With its white sands and gentle waves, Trincomalee beach is the ultimate getaway for those who desire a relaxing vacation in Sri Lanka.

Day 4: In the morning, hit back to the beautiful golden sandy beach and jump into a boat for an amazing ride on the sea to explore Pigeon Island. The island possesses a 200m long and 100m wide coral reef with over 100 species of exquisite corals making it an unmatched live coral reef, unlike any other in Sri Lanka.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Koneshwaram Kovil
  • Fort Frederick
  • Pigeon Island boat ride Snorkeling
  • Relax in beach
DAY 5 - POLONNARUWA ANCIENT CITY/ JEEP SAFARI

After breakfast, you will proceed to Polonnaruwa ancient city (UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE). Sri Lanka has a rich heritage and Polonnaruwa is one of the greatest and oldest civilizations in the world. It was the second capital, ruled by 56 kings for over 8000 years.

Afternoon, you will be heading to Minneriya/Kaudulla National Park for an afternoon 4×4 jeep safari, since it is the best time to spot wild elephants in their natural habitat. Spreading over 8800 hectares, this park is mainly a sanctuary for elephants.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Minneriya Wildlife safari
  • Ayurvedic herbal treatment can be arranged
  • Bicycle tour around the ancient city – Included
DAY 6 - SIGIRIYA ROCK FORTRESS / VILLAGE TOUR

After breakfast, you will take a wonderful journey across the stunning Sri Lankan countryside towards Dambulla city. Visit Sigiriya Lion Rock Fortress (UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE). Sigiriya refuses to reveal its secrets easily; in fact, you have to climb a series of vertiginous staircases to reach the top.

Thereafter, you will enjoy a wonderful cultural village tour while exploring the beautiful countryside. It will start with a Bullock Cart ride, one of the most ancient and prime transportation systems in Sri Lanka. While on the tour you will have a canoeing boat ride across the lake and a TukTuk drive before reaching the farmer’s house.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Sigiriya Ancient Rock Fortress visit
  • Catamaran boat ride – Included in the package
  • Cultural Village Tour – Included in the package
DAY 7 - CAVE TEMPLE / KANDY CITY TOUR

Early in the morning, climb Pidurangala rock to capture the morning sunrise. Afterwards, enjoy breakfast at the hotel and set off to Kandy. En route, visit the Golden Buddha Statue, the Royal Cave Temple (UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE), and Matale Spice Garden.

Then, proceed towards Kandy city, the capital of the last Sinhalese kingdom. Kandy is famous for its sacred Buddhist sites, including the Temple of the Tooth Relic (UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE). Thereafter, you’ll enjoy a walk around Kandy’s busy city centre and engage with a cultural dance show.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Pidurangala rock hike
  • Dambulla cave temple visit
  • Temple of Tooth Relic visit
  • Hot-air balloon tour can be arranged
  • Kandy city tour (local markets/view point)
  • Spice garden visit – Included in the package
  • Traditional dance show – Included in the package
DAY 8 - HILL COUNTRY TEA PLANTATION / WATERFALLS

After breakfast, you will visit Tiesh gem museum. Next, you will visit the Peradeniya Royal Garden, which is known as one of the best botanical gardens in Asia.

Thereafter, you’ll enjoy a wonderful journey through the lush green tea plantations and waterfalls towards Sri Lanka’s tea region of Nuwara Eliya. It is often referred to as “Little England”. While driving through the cold mist hills, stop at a tea plantation and step into a tea factory to observe the rich tradition of processing Ceylon Tea.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Gem Museum
  • Peradeniya Royal Botanical Garden
  • Tea factory visit
DAY 9 - SPECTACULAR TRAIN JOURNEY

In the morning, you will enjoy a spectacular train journey from Nanu-oya station to Ella village. It is known as one of the most picturesque hill country train journeys in the world.

Ella is everyone’s favorite hill country village due to the fact that it’s a wonderful nature-friendly location. The Nine Arch Bridge, a major tourist attraction in Ella, is a massive bridge built entirely of solid rocks, bricks and cement. Ella is a stunning location that anyone who is visiting Sri Lanka should not miss out.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Mini Adam’s Peak trekking
  • Ella Village walk (Nine Arch bridge)
  • Spectacular train journey – Included in the package
DAY 10 - LEOPARDS TRAILS SAFARI AT YALA NATIONAL PARK

After breakfast, depart from Ella towards Yala National Park. En-route, you will visit Ravana waterfall, one of the most popular sightseeing attractions in Ella.

Later on, visit Yala National Park, the largest and most stunning national park in Sri Lanka. Encompassing a range of diverse ecosystems, the park has an impressive variety of wildlife, including sloth bears, leopards and lots of colorful birds. Enjoy a 4×4 jeep ride searching for wildlife under the evening sunset.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Rawana Waterfall
  • Wildlife safari at Yala National Park
DAY 11 - LEISURE DAY ON THE BEACH

After a fantastic journey through the coconut palms, mountains and waterfalls, you have finally arrived at the world-famous beachside of Sri Lanka. Mirissa/Dickwella is a delightful sandy beach characterized by white sands and coconut palms.

Upon arrival at your hotel, you may grab a towel to relax on the beach while enjoying a cocktail or a chill beer under the shade of a palm tree. Optional activities include Jet Ski, Banana & Tube Boating, Diving, Snorkeling, and Surfing.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Hummanaya Blowhole visit
  • Leisure & relax in the beach
  • Water sports can be arranged
DAY 12 - WHALE WATCHING / LEISURE DAY

Early in the morning, hit back to the beautiful golden sandy beach and jump into a boat for an amazing ride on the sea to capture the blue whales and dolphins. Mirissa is one of the best places in the world to spot the biggest creature on the planet.

With a free afternoon, you may enjoy walking around the fisherman’s village or sunbathing on the beach. Evening, you’ll definitely enjoy the stunning scenery of sunset and pleasant seaside atmosphere.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Early morning enjoy the Whale Watching Boat Ride (Seasonal Activity)
  • Leisure & relax in the beach
  • Water activities can be arranged
DAY 13 - GALLE FORT / TURTLE HATCHERY

Early in the morning, your tour guide will drive you to Galle, a city well-known for its Fort, along the southwest coast. It is the point where classical British and Dutch architecture interacts with a tropical environment.

You’ll spend the afternoon at a turtle hatchery center where young turtles are trained to swim for the first time and injured adults are rehabilitated and released back into the wild.

ACTIVITIES:
  • After breakfast proceed toward Galle
  • En route to Galle, Capture the Stilt fishermen
  • Visit a World-famous Galle Fort & nearby popular places
  • Visit a Turtle Hatchery Center
  • Evening, enjoy a Boat Ride in Madu River & Fish Foot therapy
DAY 14 - COLOMBO CITY TOUR / AIRPORT OR HOTEL DROP-OFF

After breakfast, you have the opportunity to take an optional city walk with your tour guide to visit the most highlighted places in the city and also do some last-minute shopping as well.

Perhaps, you may intend to pop into a famous and internationally renowned restaurant in Colombo to end your trip over an unforgettable meal. Thereafter, proceed to the airport in time for the return flight.

ACTIVITIES:
  • Colombo city and shopping tour
  • Colombo highlights: Galle Face Green, Gangarama Independent Square, etc…
